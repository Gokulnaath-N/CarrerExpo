Business Analyst

Business Analysts solve business problems by collaborating with stakeholders and translating requirements into functional specifications.

AR/VR Developer

AR/VR Developers create immersive experiences using AR and VR technologies. They collaborate with designers and engineers to build interactive applications for gaming, education, and training.

Civil Engineer

Civil Engineers design, construct, and maintain infrastructure projects like buildings, roads, bridges, and water supply systems. They work with architects, contractors, and other professionals to complete projects on time and within budget.

Data Scientist

Data Scientists analyze data to help organizations make informed decisions by identifying trends and patterns.

Cloud Engineer

Cloud Engineers design, implement, and manage cloud-based solutions. They collaborate with developers, architects, and other professionals to deploy cloud infrastructure that meets organizational needs.

AI/ML Engineer

AI/ML Engineers develop AI and ML algorithms to automate tasks, improve decision-making, and enhance user experiences. They work with data scientists, engineers, and other professionals to deploy AI/ML solutions that meet organizational needs.

Full Stack Developer

Full Stack Developers design, develop, and maintain web applications. They work with front-end and back-end technologies to create user-friendly applications that meet business requirements.

Cybersecurity Analyst

Cybersecurity Analysts protect organizations from cyber threats by monitoring networks, detecting vulnerabilities, and responding to security incidents.

DevOps Engineer

DevOps Engineers automate software development processes to improve efficiency, reliability, and security. They work with developers, testers, and other professionals to deploy software that meets organizational needs.

VLSI Engineer

VLSI Engineers design and develop integrated circuits for electronic devices like smartphones, computers, and IoT devices. They work with designers, engineers, and other professionals to create high-quality chips that meet performance and power requirements.

Mechanical Engineer

Mechanical Engineers design, develop, and test mechanical systems like engines, machines, and tools. They work with designers, technicians, and other professionals to create products that meet performance and safety standards.

Machine Learning Engineer

Machine Learning Engineers develop algorithms that enable machines to learn from data and make decisions. They work with data scientists, engineers, and other professionals to deploy machine learning solutions that meet organizational needs.

Gaming & Animation

Gaming & Animation professionals create interactive experiences for entertainment, education, and training. They work with designers, developers, and other professionals to produce high-quality games and animations that engage users.

UI/UX Designer

UI/UX Designers create user-friendly interfaces and experiences for websites, applications, and products. They work with developers, testers, and other professionals to design interfaces that meet user needs and business requirements.

Teaching

Teaching professionals educate students in schools, colleges, and universities. They work with students, parents, and other educators to create a positive learning environment that fosters academic growth and personal development.

Marine Engineer

Marine Engineers design, construct, and maintain ships, boats, and offshore structures. They work with naval architects, shipbuilders, and other professionals to ensure the safety and efficiency of marine vessels.
